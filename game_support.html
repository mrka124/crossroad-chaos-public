Support Center

Welcome to the support page. Our game is designed to be a simple, standalone offline experience. Most common questions are answered below.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does the game require an internet connection? No. This game is completely offline. Once downloaded, you can play anytime without Wi-Fi or mobile data.
How is my game progress saved? Progress is stored locally on your device. Please note that if you delete the app, your local save data will be removed.
I encountered a technical issue. What should I do? Since the game runs entirely on your device, most issues can be fixed by restarting the app or your device. Ensure you have enough free storage space for the game to function properly.

Privacy & Data

This application does not collect, track, or share any personal information. No user data is transmitted to external servers as there is no online component.
