Traffic Priorities

Balance everyday traffic with urgent vehicles and protected convoys. Each lane, route, and siren asks for a different kind of timing.

City Traffic Cars, taxis, trucks
Emergency Routes Ambulance and police
VIP Convoys Escorted limo routes

Risk & Pressure

Every mistake has momentum. Crashes cost lives, long queues drain points, and impatient drivers make the intersection harder to control.

Crash Limit 3 lives per run
Recovery Delay 1.5 - 2.5s after impact
Queue Pressure Drivers react after 2 cycles

Changing Conditions

Weather and time of day change how the road feels. Rain, snow, headlights, and light beacons all reshape your rhythm mid-run.

Rain Slower roads, tire spray
Snow Reduced speed, drifting flurries
Day & Night Headlights and glowing signals
